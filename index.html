🎯 About the Event

DATE: 10-11 OCTOBER 2025

VENUE: SHAMBHUNATH COLLEGE OF EDUCATION

Embark on a thrilling two-day software and hardware development hackathon where participants create innovative applications inspired by given themes within a defined time frame and then elevate their projects to new heights.

This event provides a great opportunity for innovation! Students will form teams and solve real-world problems through coding and creative thinking.

📌 Rules & Guidelines

  • Maximum 4 members in a team
  • Bring your college ID card during the event
  • Every team must have their own laptop
  • 24 hours to complete your project
  • Innovative and problem-solving ideas will be given priority
  • Selected teams must have all members present
  • Respect all participants and maintain a positive environment
  • Original code only - no plagiarism allowed

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I participate alone?
No, you must participate in this event as a team. Teams must consist of 2 to 4 members.
Can people from outside the college participate?
This event is primarily for college students, but alumni can participate with special permission
Do we need to choose our problem statement in advance?
yes, you can choose the problem statement on the day of the event. However, you can think of ideas in advance.
What technologies can we use for our project?
You can use any technology stack you're comfortable with - web development, mobile apps, AI/ML, IoT, or anything that solves the problem effectively.
